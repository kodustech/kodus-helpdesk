## Statuses
- Open → In Progress → Resolved → Closed

## Ticket Creation
- Customer users (all roles) can create tickets in their workspace
- Internal users (Owner, Admin, Editor) can create tickets on behalf of clients

## Closing Rules
- Internal users (Owner, Admin, Editor): must provide a reason when closing a ticket
- Customer users: can close without providing a reason
